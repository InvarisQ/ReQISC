Invaris Quantum ReQISC

Invaris Quantum ReQISC is an open-source foundation for building, testing, and benchmarking reconfigurable quantum instruction-set components across hardware-aware compiler and control workflows.

Current status

The project is an early scaffold with typed primitives, examples, tests, documentation, and contribution pathways. It does not claim production compiler maturity or benchmark performance evidence.
